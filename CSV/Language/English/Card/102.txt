Select one monster on the opponent's field and discard one Fire Attribute monster card from your hand: Inflict damage to your opponent equal to half of the discarded monster's attack power.\n[2]. When this card is destroyed by effect damage from a Fire Attribute card and sent to the Graveyard: Draw one card from your Deck.
