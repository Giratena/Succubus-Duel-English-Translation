If your LP is 3000 or less;  Activate when an opponent declares an attack. Grant [Charm] and [Betrayal] to the attacking character. If the attacked character has a [Lip] card equipped; additionally inflict [Kiss Addiction (Permanent)].\n[2]. At the end of your opponent's second turn after this card was activated; banish this card.
